# FMQSMO
The U.S. Department of the Treasury, Financial Management Quality Service Management Office (FM QSMO) continues market research to obtain industry feedback on the revised draft concepts and vision for the FM QSMO Marketplace. This virtual repository represents the Reading Room for the FM QSMO Marketplace Request for Information (RFI). It houses several artifacts referenced throughout the updated/refined FM QSMO Marketplace Vision (Attachment 1), namely components of the Financial Management Capability Framework (FMCF). For convenience, a copy of the updated/refined FM QSMO Marketplace Vision is also included in this repository.  

  

Information provided in this RFI, including the updated/refined Vision and associated artifacts, remains subject to change.  Nothing in this RFI should be construed as a final government decision upon which industry should take action.  Industry feedback is being sought solely for market research and planning purposes.  This RFI does not imply an obligation on the part of the government to proceed with any solicitation for products/services, nor to ultimately pursue a particular acquisition strategy. All information received in response to this RFI marked as proprietary will be handled accordingly.
